Redae Foundation is a humanitarian organization dedicated to supporting vulnerable communities across Ethiopia. We believe that every individual deserves the chance to live with dignity, access essential resources, and build a sustainable future. Our mission is to provide practical assistance, empower individuals, and create opportunities for education, skill-building, family reconnection, and employment.

At Redae, we believe humanitarian work must balance immediate response with long-term empowerment. While emergency aid such as food, clothing, and medical support is essential, it is not enough on its own. Real impact comes from restoring stability, reconnecting families, enabling access to livelihoods, and creating opportunities for independence.
